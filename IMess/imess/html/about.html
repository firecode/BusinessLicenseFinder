IEEE MEeting Support System will enable collaboration of Meeting Organizers and Attendees of the meeting and facilitates smooth meeting planning.

There are four categories of users for the system namely Administrators, Meeting Organizers, Session Organizers, and Attendees. The Meeting Organizers and Session Organizers are considered the super users of the meeting set up. The Attendees are considered as the end users of the system. The Administrator is not directly related to the collaboration and manages the system.
